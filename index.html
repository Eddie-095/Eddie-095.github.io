About D'Sauce

Our Story

Founded in 2015, D'Sauce began as a small food stall with a big dream - to serve the most flavorful, saucy and spicy fast food in Nigeria.

What started as a passion project has now grown into one of the most beloved fast food brands in the region, known for our signature sauces and perfectly spiced dishes.

Every dish at D'Sauce is prepared with care, using only the freshest ingredients and our secret blend of spices that keep customers coming back for more.

Our Team

At the heart of D'Sauce is our dedicated team of chefs and staff who work tirelessly to ensure every customer enjoys an exceptional dining experience.

Our chefs undergo rigorous training to master the art of preparing our signature dishes while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and food safety.
